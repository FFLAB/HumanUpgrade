About

Welcome to Human Upgrade, we are a new bionic company. Aiming to develop the most technological and stylish prosthetics at the most affordable price. Founded in 2017 by Peter Simmes, Human Upgrade has come a long way from its beginnigs in Chicago. When Peter first started as a engineer graduated in Robotics and Medical Biotechnology at the Hamilton State University of Technology, he found himself charmed by the world of bionic prosthetics and how technology and biology could co-exist in harmony to improve the lifes of those who need it. With the help of an awesome team and the support of the Human Upgrade community, we’re pioneering a new bionic age inspired by science fiction.
